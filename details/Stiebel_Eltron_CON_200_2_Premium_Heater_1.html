Stiebel Eltron CON 200-2 Premium Convection Heater

Looking for a modern and efficient heating solution that blends style with performance? The Stiebel Eltron CON 200-2 Premium convection heater is designed to heat your space without the hassle of noisy fans. The programmable timer with three preset options and a booster heating function adds flexibility and convenience, letting you customize your heating schedule to match your lifestyle. Plus, the open window detection system automatically adjusts heat output when windows are open, helping you save energy and reduce costs.
