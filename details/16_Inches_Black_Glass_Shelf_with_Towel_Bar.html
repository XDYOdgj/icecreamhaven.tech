Modern 16-inch Black Glass Shelf

Enhance your bathroom with a sleek and functional 16-inch black glass shelf that blends style with practicality. This floating design is perfect for keeping your bath essentials, such as shampoo, conditioner, soap, and towels, neatly organized and easily accessible. With a strong weight capacity of 44 lbs, it handles daily use with ease.

Constructed from durable SUS 304 stainless steel and tempered glass, this shelf offers both safety and long-lasting performance. The tempered glass not only adds a touch of elegance but also shatters into smaller, less dangerous pieces, making it a secure choice for your bathroom. Its simple, shiny finish complements modern decor and brings a refined look to any space.

Installation is effortless with included hardware and clear instructions. Just four drilled holes are needed, and the adjustable hardware lets you customize the spacing for maximum convenience. The extra-long towel bar doubles as a great towel holder, increasing storage capacity and functionality in one sleek design. Ideal for both style and practical use, this shelf is a smart upgrade that enhances your daily bathroom routine.
