Modern Lighting for Every Room

The TuyuBEE 5 Color Temperature LED Track Lighting Kit is designed to bring both style and functionality to your space. With its 12W COB track lighting heads, you can enjoy a powerful 1000 lumen output—matching the brightness of a 70W halogen bulb, but with significantly more efficiency and longer lifespan. The adjustable zoom feature lets you easily control the light spread, making it ideal for both focused spotlighting and broad ambient lighting.

Equipped with dimming capabilities, this track lighting system allows you to adjust the brightness from 10% to 100%, giving you the perfect lighting for any mood, occasion, or time of day. The built-in color temperature switch provides five different options: 2700K (warm white), 3000K (soft white), 4000K (natural white), 5000K (daylight), and 6000K (cool white), so you can tailor the lighting to complement your interior design and create a welcoming atmosphere.

Designed for quick and easy installation, this kit includes all the necessary components: track rails, canopy connectors, end caps, and screws. The sleek, contemporary look of the track light is perfect for modern kitchens, living rooms, galleries, or retail displays. Plus, its high CRI 90+ ensures vibrant, natural color rendering, making it ideal for both residential and commercial settings where lighting clarity and aesthetics are key.
